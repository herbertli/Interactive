Assignment 7 - Tracking

Herbert Li

About

Being the middle of finals season, for this assignment I tried to implement the first thing that came to mind. I finally got around to watching Stranger Things so I immediately thought of the supernatural/paranormal. So, I decided to implement a motion tracking game where a user can swat down incoming UFOs, using any motion that can be picked up by the camera. The UFOs move using Perlin noise, and when hit, they explode, gradually fading into the background. The detection has a bit of lag time, so try and aim for slow, deliberate movements.
